GUILD LINE

1. Parachuting is subject to favorable weather conditions, and the Pilot may cancel the parachuting at any time.

2. For comfortable parachuting, wear comfortable shoes and dress casually.

3. Pregnant women are not advised to parachute.

4. Prefer not to transport children under the age of six.

5. A typical Sri Lanka parachuting experience lasts about an hour. However, real-time is dependent on weather conditions and landing area availability.

6. Before each jump, go over your emergency procedures.

7. Approach the plane from the back.

8. Before exiting, perform handle checks.

9. At break off, be extremely aware of everyone around you.

10. Be mentally prepared for malfunctions and always have your emergency procedures ready.
